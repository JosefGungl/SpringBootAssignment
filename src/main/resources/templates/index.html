Our lives are getting busier - and more interrupted - than ever. Keeping focus and getting those millions of tings done is a big challenge. I studied a lot of books and techniques to solve this problem. I personally found them way to complicated and practically useless quite immediately. I ended up designing a flow that has two main goals.

1.

Be extremely simple to understand, follow and implement for everyone.

2.

Make you feel accomplished at the end of each day, instead of constantly stressed out.
