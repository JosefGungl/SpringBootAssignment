Step 2

Plan the day out

Now that you have a good list of things in your 'to-do' list, pick the important ones you want to focus on TODAY. Not today and tomorrow, not this week, but just TODAY. It's important or else you could become overwhelmed by the list. Even if you know where you want to start, you won't be able to finish them all. Not because you are unproductive or incapable, but because we have limited time in our busy days. There is nothing we can do about that so let's plan around this issue.

Depending on the complexity, pick about five of them and try to get those done. The difference is that now you will more likely have your list cleared at the end of the day. You will feel more accomplished and 'in control' instead of stressed out and hopeless.

WHAT TO DO: In the morning, pick a limited number of tasks to do TODAY. Keep all of this logged in a notebook or in the notes section of your phone. The most important thing is to stay consistent and organized.
